Tek Community center

community centers are a common setting in most cities. The difference with this community center is that it combines what is found in the common setting of community centers and ads a more commonplace social setting by mixing it with an electronic store that fixes computers/electronics at a great discount.

One reason to combine the two is to still provide the same resources of normal community centers and to include the addition of a tech center at large discounts. People who really need computer maintenance can also continue to gain a discount by attending computer building classes and assist with their own repairs.
